Education

Here, I was Vice President of my school’s Girls Who Code chapter, and Vice President of the South Asian student association. I was also an active member of DECA, FBLA, National Honor Society, and Spanish National Honor Society, and played on the Girls Tennis team.

Over the summer of 2019, I was chosen from a pool of applicants to join the Program in Algorithmic and Combinatorial Thinking, where I studied a course equivalent to CIS 160 at the University of Pennsylvania. On campus, I am involved with the Women in Science and Engineering, Ethical Investments Club, Elementary Engineering Partnerships, and the Indian American Student Association. I plan to graduate in May of 2025.

Projects

Mindhub: App Prototype

This summer, I had the opportunity to ideate, design, and prototype an app as part of a selective program held by Apple and Kode with Klossy.

My app, Mindhub, is a mental health platform that seeks to confronts the stigma around seeking help for mental health in the South Asian community. Mindhub also allows users to receive help from those that share their social identity, since modern therapy is often too westernized to be inclusive of all communities and cultures.

Mindhub offers:
Ⅰ. Scheduled, 1-1 conversations with trained South Asian counselors
ⅠⅠ. Immediate live-chat option with trained South Asian counselors
ⅠⅠⅠ. Opportunity to explore & create articles and artwork about your personal experience
